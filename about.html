Background

I know what broken looks like.
And how to fix it.

I am an AAT Licensed Accountant and MAAT qualified professional with over 15 years of experience working inside and alongside UK accounting practices — across hospitality, manufacturing and distribution.

I hold the CIMA professional qualification at finalist stage and an MSc in Accountancy and Finance. That academic and professional grounding gives me something that compliance-only accountants do not have: the ability to think strategically about a business, not just account for it.

I started Reckon Management in 2022, building a delivery team capable of handling volume production work — bookkeeping, VAT, payroll, management accounts — so that I can focus on what adds the most value: the complex transformation work, the director-level advisory, the situations where a business needs someone who genuinely understands both the numbers and the operation.

I have walked into a hotel group with 20 entities and no proper bookkeeping, and fixed it. I have been embedded in a distribution business since 2022, involved in credit control, operations and commercial decisions — not just month-end reconciliations. I have been sub-contracted by chartered accountants for complex manufacturing clients because of the technical depth I bring.

That is the experience behind every engagement we take on.
