Sector 01

Hospitality & property

Hotel groups and property portfolios present some of the most complex accounting challenges of any sector — multi-entity structures, intercompany transactions, mixed trading and property income, and directors who need clarity across the whole group. This is where we have our deepest case study.

Multi-entity group structures

Holding companies, property SPVs and trading entities each with their own obligations — but all needing to reconcile as a group. Intercompany charges, loan accounts and consolidated reporting done correctly.

Property income and compliance

Rental income, service charges, lease accounting and VAT on property — the rules are complex and the penalties for getting it wrong are significant. We know property finance.

Operational hotel finance

Revenue management, departmental P&L, food and beverage margin, payroll complexity. We understand the operational numbers behind a hotel — not just the statutory accounts.

Director-level visibility

Hotel group directors need to see across the whole portfolio — which entities are performing, where cash is, what obligations are coming. We provide that consolidated clarity.

Sector 02

Manufacturing & FMCG

Manufacturers — particularly food and FMCG businesses — have finance requirements that generic accountants struggle with. Production costing, stock valuation, HMRC compliance and making sure the people running the system actually understand it. We have worked directly in this sector and understand its specific demands.

Sage for manufacturing

Sage is the dominant platform in UK manufacturing. We have set it up, audited it and trained staff on it. We know where the common errors are — and how to fix them without disrupting operations.

Staff training and capability

A system is only as good as the people using it. We train finance teams to use their software correctly — not just to follow a process, but to understand what they are doing and why.

Reconciliation and accuracy

Manufacturing businesses often have significant stock, supplier and customer balances. Reconciliation errors compound quickly. We clear backlogs and establish processes that keep things clean going forward.

Referred by chartered accountants

We are sub-contracted by qualified chartered accountants for complex manufacturing clients. That peer trust reflects the technical depth we bring — not just compliance management.

Sector 03

Distribution

Distribution businesses have complex financial dynamics — credit control, related party transactions, stock management, supplier terms, regulatory compliance. We have been embedded in a distribution business since 2022, working not just on the finance but across the operations. We understand how distribution businesses actually work.

Credit control and debtor management

Distribution margins are tight — and slow-paying customers destroy cash flow. We manage credit control properly, not as an afterthought. Aged debtor reporting, escalation processes and customer risk assessment.

Related party transactions

Many distribution businesses have complex related party relationships — between directors, associated companies, and connected persons. These need to be correctly identified, documented and disclosed.

Operational finance involvement

The best finance support in distribution is not just month-end accounts — it is being involved in the commercial decisions. Pricing analysis, margin by product line, supplier terms. We go beyond the books.

Regulatory compliance

Sponsor licence compliance, import/export documentation, HMRC obligations specific to distribution. We handle the full compliance picture, not just the accounts.
