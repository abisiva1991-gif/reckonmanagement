About us

15 years inside UK practices.
Now working directly for you.

I spent 15 years working inside UK accounting practices — handling accounts, tax, payroll and advisory work for businesses of every size and sector. I know how good accounting support should feel, and I built Reckon Management to deliver exactly that.

What sets us apart is the combination of UK-qualified expertise and a dedicated support team. I lead every client relationship and review all work personally. Behind the scenes, our experienced accounting team ensures nothing is delayed — even at the busiest times of year.
