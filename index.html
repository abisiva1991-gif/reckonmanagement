The problem we solve

Most businesses outgrow their finance function before they realise it.

The signs are always there. Accounts that cannot be trusted. Reports that arrive too late. Directors making decisions without the numbers to back them. We fix the root cause — not just the symptoms.

Bookkeeping that doesn't reflect reality
Transactions recorded incorrectly, intercompany charges missing, software barely used. The numbers exist — but they cannot be trusted.
Multi-entity structures with no clear oversight
Holding companies, property SPVs, trading entities — each with their own obligations, all somehow not talking to each other.
Finance staff without the knowledge to run it
Good people in the wrong roles, using software they have not been trained on, producing reports that do not get used.
Directors making decisions without financial clarity
Year-end not set up. VAT not filed. No management accounts. Strategic decisions made without the numbers to back them.
