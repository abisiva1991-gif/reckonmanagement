Compliance

Year-end accounts

Your statutory accounts prepared accurately and filed at Companies House on time, every year. We handle the full process from pulling your records together to final submission — so you never have to worry about a deadline or a penalty.

We work with your accounting software directly, review everything personally, and provide you with a clear summary of your financial position at year end.

What's included
  • Statutory accounts preparation (sole trader, partnership or limited company)
  • Companies House filing
  • Director's report and notes
  • Year-end review and summary
  • Liaison with HMRC on your behalf
Compliance

Corporation tax

Your corporation tax return prepared accurately and submitted to HMRC well before the deadline. We don't just complete the form — we review your position and identify every relief and allowance your company is entitled to.

With 15 years of UK practice experience, we know where savings are typically missed and make sure you're not overpaying.

What's included
  • CT600 preparation and HMRC submission
  • Tax computation and liability calculation
  • Capital allowances review
  • R&D tax credit assessment (where applicable)
  • Tax planning recommendations
Tax

VAT returns & Making Tax Digital

Your VAT return prepared, reviewed and submitted to HMRC every quarter. We're fully Making Tax Digital compliant and handle everything — including Xero, QuickBooks or Sage integration — so your submissions are accurate and on time.

MTD for Income Tax is expanding in 2026. We ensure every client is set up and ready well ahead of any new requirements.

What's included
  • Quarterly VAT return preparation and submission
  • MTD-compliant software setup and management
  • VAT registration and deregistration
  • Partial exemption and reverse charge handling
  • HMRC correspondence management
Tax

Self-assessment tax returns

Personal tax returns completed for company directors, sole traders, landlords and anyone with income outside PAYE. We make sure you pay exactly what you owe — and we proactively look for ways to reduce your tax liability legally.

What's included
  • Self-assessment registration and filing
  • Employment, dividend and rental income
  • Capital gains tax calculations
  • Pension contributions and reliefs
  • Payment on account planning
Operations

Payroll

Monthly payroll processed accurately, every month. PAYE submissions made to HMRC, payslips issued to your team and pension contributions calculated and reported. We make sure your obligations are always met on time.

What's included
  • Monthly payroll processing
  • PAYE and NIC calculations
  • RTI submissions to HMRC
  • Payslip production
  • Auto-enrolment pension management
  • P60 and P11D year-end reporting
Advisory

Management accounts

Monthly or quarterly management accounts that tell you how your business is actually performing — not just what happened last year. Profit and loss, cashflow, balance sheet and plain-English commentary you can act on immediately.

This is the service that turns your accountant from a compliance function into a genuine business partner. With our CIMA-trained background, we bring management accounting expertise that most small firm accountants can't offer.

What's included
  • Monthly or quarterly P&L, balance sheet and cashflow
  • KPI dashboard and variance analysis
  • Rolling cashflow forecast
  • Commentary and recommendations
  • Quarterly review call
